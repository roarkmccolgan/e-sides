Objectives

Objective 1
Identify ethical and societal issues of privacy-preserving big data technologies
Objective 2
Validate privacy-preserving big data technologies
Objective 3
Build a community of big data stakeholders
Objective 4
Enhance discussion, learning and networking in the community
Objective 5
Develop recommendations to facilitate Responsible Research and Innovation around big data technologies
Objective 6
Report on ethical and societal issues related to big data
Objective 7
Present he final recommendations in a community position paper

Phases

Phase 1

Create Awareness  

Duration: M1-M12
Main Goals:
  • Raising awareness about the project
  • Building a stakeholders’ community
Activities and tools
  • Webinars
  • Organization of events and participation in external events
  • Production of white papers
Phase 2

Increase the potential Impact

Duration: M12-M24
Main Goals:
  • Increase community involvement
  • Disseminate the results
  • Development of preliminary recommendations
Activities and tools
  • Webinars
  • Organization of events and participation in external events
  • Production of white papers
Phase 3

Results

Duration: M25-M34
Main Goals:
  • Building consensus and validate the project results and recommendations
  • Raising participation of community in events and on the platform
Activities and tools
  • Organization of events and participation in external events
  • Publish materials on the online platform
  • Publications on targeted online media
Phase 4

Valorization 

Duration: M34-M36+>36
Main Goals:
  • Disseminating the final recommendations
  • Demonstrating the relevance of privacy-preserving big data technologies
Activities and tools
  • Demonstrations for specific audiences: presentation of final results
  • Publishing the white papers and community paper in national/international journals and online media
